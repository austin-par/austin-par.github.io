Product

We believe the operations of mentor programs can be broken down into four main stages: recruiting, training, matching and facilitating. The aspect of the operations that we focus on improving through technology is the matching. From our research, we have discovered that there are five essential tasks in the matching of mentors and mentees. They are:

  • collection of user information
  • secure storage of that information
  • understanding what attributes allow for successful mentor-mentee relationships
  • matching of these pairs
  • facilitating of their relationship throughout the program.

Our ideal customer is someone who wants to impact the community.
